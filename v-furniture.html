Furniture Models & Planning

I create 3D Models and 2D manufacturing-ready plans for furniture, jewelry or other items,that can be used to help a builder fabricate them, or to help you better understand what will work for your needs.

From my experience creating models and drawings for my own projects, I can ensure that the documentation we create is realistic and manufacturable.

Example: Standing Desk

Here is an example of a standing desk I designed for a friend's home office. We started with a rough sketch that he had made. From that I created a full 3D model so that we could explore design options, and then created detailed 2D plans that a local woodworker could use to fabricate the desk.

I use Onshape for most CAD modeling, and it has a built-in feature in the mobile app that allows a user to place a model in space with augmented reality. In this case, we were able to put the standing desk in a room to see how it fit—a short video showing this functionality is included at the bottom of the page.

This short video shows the augmented reality (AR) feature called AR View, which is native to Onshape's mobile app (and free), with shows the standing desk model placed against a wall.

Augmented Reality enables the simple overlay of digital content onto a physical space.
