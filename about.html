Peter A. Zink, PhD

About

I design and build technical solutions for problems in sustainable energy, manufacturing, and engineering. I leverage decades of experience across academia, industry and government to solve complex problems, including:

  • Research reactor and electrochemical sensor development for high-temperature electrochemical materials testing and characterization.
  • Engineering design and workforce development
  • Marine electronics and electrical propulsion systems
  • Industrial robotics visualization with augmented reality
  • Affordable medical device design and prototyping
  • Building construction and rehabilitation

Professional Background

Decades of engineering experience including high-temperature Solid Oxide Fuel Cell research and molten salt reactor design, engineering curriculum design and teaching, automated manufacturing systems design, digital twin development with augmented reality and the internet of things, medical device prototyping, historic post-and-beam barn construction and rehabilitation, and 3D visualization.

Technical Expertise

Hands-on skills in mechanical and manufacturing systems, PLC and industrial robot programming, high-temperature electrochemistry, coding (JavaScript, Python, IDL, Arduino), Computer-Aided Design (CAD), and fine woodworking.

I also built this website, and have built dozens of others. AI agents like Anthropic's Claude have lowered the barrier for programming. I recently "vibe-coded" iOS and macOS apps with Swift UI with the help of Claude. It's not perfect, but it's a powerful tool that can help accelerate development.

Academics

I have been a lecturer and academic advisor and curriculum author at several universities, with experience in designing and delivering engineering education programs, in-person and online. I'm honored to hear from former students, who reach out for career advice and just to stay in touch.
