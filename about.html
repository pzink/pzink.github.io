Peter A. Zink, PhD

About

I manage, consult, and build technical solutions in sustainable energy, manufacturing, and engineering design. Over 25 years of experience across academia, industry and government, I have developed a wide range of solutions to complex problems, including:

  • Research reactor designs for high-temperature electrochemical materials separation
  • Engineering design and workforce development curriculum
  • Marine electronics and electrical propulsion systems design and installation
  • Industrial robotics visualization with augmented reality
  • Medical device design and prototyping
  • Historic barn construction and rehabilitation

Professional Background

25 years of engineering experience including high-temperature Solid Oxide Fuel Cell research and molten salt reactor design, engineering curriculum design and teaching, automated manufacturing systems design, digital twin development with augmented reality and the internet of things, medical device prototyping, historic post-and-beam barn construction and rehabilitation, and 3D visualization.

Technical Expertise

Hands-on skills in mechanical and manufacturing systems, PLC and industrial robot programming, high-temperature electrochemistry, coding (JavaScript, Python, IDL, Arduino), Computer-Aided Design (CAD), and fine woodworking.

Academics

Lecturer and academic advisor with experience in designing and delivering engineering education programs, in-person and online. I'm honored to still hear from former students to this day, who reach out for career advice.
