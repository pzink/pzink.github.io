Woodworking

I've been doing woodwork for most of my life, as a part-time job, as a full-time business owner, and as a hobbiest. I prefer starting from Roz son taking those raw boards through all of the steps to a finish project. I find great satisfaction and tight joinery, things made without nails or screws. I have volunteered and been a paid consultant for post and beam barn raising and restoration, and the strength of these structures in spite of the lack of metal hardware is quality I like to incorporate in the furniture I make as well, where possible.

The collection here represents just a few of the things I've made over the years.
