Legal Visualization

From accident reconstructions to property boundary illustrations, I can create compelling and clear visualizations for legal cases where the physical and topological information is critical to understanding the nature of the case.

In two dimensions or three, I can create approximate or very accurate models of spaces, objects, etc. I can overlay certified plot plans, images, photos and drawings and add 3-D information to them to highlight features or clarify distances, locations, etc.

Where the real world geometry is poorly documented and highly complex, I can also take LiDAR scans of the area and turn them into CAD models, where other information and objects can be inserted and clarified.
